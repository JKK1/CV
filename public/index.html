Profile

Independent technical expert in digital-asset matters.

I am a mathematician and engineer with first-hand operational experience in cryptocurrency, blockchain protocols, mining infrastructure and algorithmic trading. I provide independent expert testimony and technical analysis for litigation, arbitration, regulatory and internal-investigation matters where the facts depend on how digital-asset systems actually work.

As founder and director of PowerPool Mining SL, a cryptocurrency mining operation with over $70M in revenue, and as project lead at Lokotech AS, where I lead the in-house design of the ASIC and the overall system architecture for our own crypto-mining hardware, I have built, operated and audited the systems most often at issue in disputes: mining pools, ASIC fleets, custodial wallets, smart contracts, exchange flows and on-chain transaction histories. Earlier work included algorithmic arbitrage of cryptocurrencies and computational-power markets, giving me a working understanding of market microstructure and trading behaviour.

My role as an expert is to translate complex technical evidence into clear, defensible findings that judges, arbitrators, juries and counsel can act on. I work — including the production of written expert reports — in Spanish, English, Italian and Catalan, and accept instructions internationally.
